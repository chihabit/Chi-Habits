the first three steps

• • • • •

"Like people say, a 21-day period is short enough to commit to, but long enough to create a lasting habit."

• • • • •

Step 1: Prepare goals and plans.
Step 2: Follow plan and review.
Step 3: Conclude and celebrate!

Step 1: Preparation Days
9 Days to Learn, Set Goals and Plan

From the time you decide to use this Workbook Journal to completion is estimated to be a month of 30 days.

Take this nine-day period to get into Day 0, which is the time that you will be all set to start the first of the 21 days.

In these days of preparation you will learn:

  • The Basic "8-Chi Habits" Overview: Take an easy 3-4 days to have an experiential understanding of the power of the 8-Chi Habits by watching the videos and trying it out.
  • The Target Basic Habits: Chi Stances and the two Heart Kung Fu of Journaling and Taichi Thinking Take 4-5 days to learn, try out and experience how these two habits can establish your habit learning and development.
Take the nine days to review the materials while you enjoy doing the activities and testing your understanding, attitude and skills needed to go through the 21-day program.

• • • • •

Step 2: Day 1-21: Activity Days
Follow Plan and Keep on Track

This is it, as soon as you embark on Day 1, you are in process along with many environmental and psychological forces that would come into play. It is game time!

  • Daily Track Your Compliance: Did you do it? How well?
  • Journal Daily Experiences: What happened? Be Reminded & Inspired.

• • • • •

Step 3: Day 21: Completion
Conclude, Celebrate and Continue

In this day you will come to a realization of the value of each day, each hour and each moment in the short 21-day journey!

Put an ending remark on your 21-day learning. Summarize.

Congratulate yourself with a small celebration and look forward to continuing your journey!
