This Workbook Journal is a 21-day, bite-sized, application of modified healing and martial spirit of Tai Chi and Qigong, programmed as daily life practices within one's busy lifestyle to become effortless habits after 21-Days.

This book centers on Chi or life energy. It talks to busy people who have affinity for Tai Chi and Qigong and an inner resonance with being a Warrior for Wellbeing.

It will guide you from beginning to the end of your journey. It includes PART 1: 9 day preparation, that will explain what you need to do for the 21 day challenge, PART 2: the 21 day chi habit journal, where you would record the accounts of your 21 day experience.

He learned from Master David Chan, the Hsin-I Liu He Ba Fa, from Master Li Jun Feng, the Chen and Yang styles of Tai Chi Chuan, Master Paul Lam, Tai Chi for Arthritis, Master Stephen Wu, Qigong, Master Saar Herman, Dao Yin Bao Jang Gong.
